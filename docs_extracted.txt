Dataset Enhancement: Synthetic Anomaly Injection
8 / 15


Why Synthetic Anomalies?

Real telecom fraud data is limited

Imbalanced datasets reduce ML performance

Synthetic anomalies simulate real-world abnormal behavior


Injection Process

Injected ~5% anomalous records

Modified: call durations, charges, international usage, activity patterns

Added column: synthetic_anomaly (1=anomaly, 0=normal)


Benefits

Improved dataset quality

Better anomaly learning

More realistic fraud simulation

Enhanced ML model robustness
