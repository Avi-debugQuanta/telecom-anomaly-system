Introduction
2 / 15


About the Study
The telecom industry generates massive volumes of usage & billing data daily.
Revenue leakage is caused by:
Billing inaccuracies
Fraudulent activities
Abnormal usage behavior
Roaming discrepancies & customer churn
Traditional rule-based systems are inefficient in detecting hidden anomalies.
AI & ML can improve revenue assurance by identifying suspicious patterns automatically.

Importance of Study

Helps telecom operators reduce financial losses

Improves operational efficiency

Enables proactive fraud & anomaly detection
Revenue Leakage Costs Telecom ~$25B Globally Per Year

Dataset Enhancement: Synthetic Anomaly Injection
8 / 15


Why Synthetic Anomalies?

Real telecom fraud data is limited

Imbalanced datasets reduce ML performance

Synthetic anomalies simulate real-world abnormal behavior


Injection Process

Injected ~5% anomalous records

Modified: call durations, charges, international usage, activity patterns

Added column: synthetic_anomaly (1=anomaly, 0=normal)


Benefits

Improved dataset quality

Better anomaly learning

More realistic fraud simulation

Enhanced ML model robustness
