SOP-01: BYPASS FRAUD
If a customer shows unusually high call minutes with zero or negligible revenue,
1. Verify call detail records (CDR) for cloned SIM or international bypass.
2. Place a temporary service bar on the line.
3. Notify the Fraud Management Team for further investigation.
4. If fraud is confirmed, issue a credit adjustment and block the offending device.

SOP-02: BILLING SYSTEM ERRORS
When total billed amount deviates >30% from expected usage‑based revenue:
1. Run a usage‑to‑billing reconciliation report.
2. Check for rating engine mis‑configuration or tariff mapping errors.
3. Correct the tariff plan in the billing system and re‑bill the affected period.
4. Document the root cause and update the SOP wiki.

SOP-03: ROAMING REVENUE LEAKAGE
If roaming usage spikes without corresponding roaming charges:
1. Validate roaming partner agreements and TAP file ingestion.
2. Ensure the mediation layer is correctly applying roaming rates.
3. Raise a ticket with the Clearing House if partner data is missing.
4. Apply provisional charges and settle post‑reconciliation.

SOP-04: PREPAID TOP‑UP FRAUD
Detect patterns of rapid successive top‑ups followed by immediate high‑value usage:
1. Flag the MSISDN for velocity checks.
2. Temporarily suspend further top‑ups until verification.
3. Conduct KYC re‑validation of the subscriber.
4. Refund any illicit top‑up amount and close the loop with the fraud team.
